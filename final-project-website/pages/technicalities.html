Data sources

Our analyses are based on the NYPD Moter Vehicle Accidents data set. This dataset is made freely available by the City of New York.

The weather data we have used is made freely available by request to the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration of U.S. Department of Commerce (NOAA) NOAA weather data.
